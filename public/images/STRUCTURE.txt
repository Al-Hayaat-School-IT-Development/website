public/images layout (see also scripts/migrate-image-paths.mjs for path rewrites)

  logo/              Brand + favicons + site.webmanifest (unchanged convention)
  shared/
    ui/              Icons & small UI: Polygon-46.svg, list-check.svg, file.png, pdf.png, bullet.png
    backgrounds/     Full-bleed textures: cta-bg*, glitter-bg*, dashlines* (+ responsive PNG variants)
    decor/           Ornamental shapes: circles, dotted.png, decor-about-*, Framedots, ellipses, polygons, etc.
  home/              Home-only marketing assets: hero feature photo + srcsets, growth photo, support-mission classroom photo (classroom-whiteboard-student*), collaborators, islam-written
    why/             Home “Why” section vector icons (vector-1..4.webp)
  payments/          Payment method marks (Stripe checkout UI)
  admissions/        Admissions hero banner (+ srcset variants)
  overlays/          Decorative frame illustrations (Frame-1362791621.webp, etc.)
  photos/            Section-scoped photography (replace-in-place friendly), e.g. about/hero-carousel/, about/team/, about/board/, curriculum/carousel/, curriculum/educators/; _legacy/ for unreferenced exports

When adding assets: pick the folder that matches usage; keep filenames stable so content JSON and next/image references stay simple.
